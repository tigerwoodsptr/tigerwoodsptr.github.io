Chuka Beach

Beautiful tourist destination with bamboo huts and tree houses. Best visited between October and April.

Siphon Canal Point

Unique system of two canals flowing one above the other - Kharja Canal and Khiri Branch.

Sunset Point

Perfect spot for photographers to capture picturesque frames at dusk.

Jhand Taal

Scenic pool named after local vegetation, located on Mahof-Barahi range border.

Lal Pul

Historic red clay bridge built during British period on Sharda Canal.

Barahasinga Tal

Marshy grassland frequented by swamp deer (Barasingha).

Crocodile Point

Prime location for observing marsh crocodiles in their natural habitat.

Sapt Sarovar

Seven-step waterfall surrounded by Sal and Teak trees.

Bhim Tal

Scenic lake with central island offering panoramic natural views.
